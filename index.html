In the darkest part of the world, where the sun never shines, there is a forest that no one goes into. The trees drip with black slime. And if you listen closely... you can hear something breathing.
Deep in the shadows crawled the Spider Scope. A twisted purple spider with a magical telescope scope bolted to its head. The scope could see things hidden from every other creature, especially the Rock Person, who was invisible to everyone else.
Then the ground started to SHAKE. The Belly Bonker stepped out of the darkness. HUGE. Dark shaggy fur, claws that could tear through rock, and in its belly... a glowing green EYE that never blinked. That eye could see almost anything. But there was ONE thing it could never see: the Rock Person.
One terrible night, the Belly Bonker came crashing through the trees! Its belly eye burned angry red. It wanted to DESTROY the Spider Scope! It swung its massive claws. Trees shattered. The Spider Scope tried to run, but the Belly Bonker was too fast!
But one of the trees behind it started to move. Bark cracked. Eyes appeared. Then a mouth. Then ANOTHER mouth. Then THREE mouths, all full of teeth sharp as swords. The Tree Surpriser. And it was STARVING.
The Spider Scope aimed its magical scope into the darkness and found the Rock Person hiding right next to the Belly Bonker! "HELP ME!" screamed the Spider Scope. The Rock Person could hear everything, but the Belly Bonker couldn't see it. The Rock Person was completely invisible.
SURPRISE!!! The Tree Surpriser opened ALL THREE of its horrible mouths and CHOMPED down on the Belly Bonker! The whole Belly Bonker disappeared inside the tree! Swallowed whole! The forest went quiet. The Spider Scope thought it was safe...

But then... the Tree Surpriser started to shake.
The Rock Person rolled right under the Belly Bonker's feet! The Belly Bonker couldn't see it coming. It TRIPPED and went flying through the air, crashing straight into the biggest tree in the forest. But that wasn't a tree... it was the Tree Surpriser. And the Tree Surpriser was VERY, VERY hungry...
Something was WRONG. Inside the Tree Surpriser, the Belly Bonker's eye glowed brighter and brighter. It wasn't dying. It was getting BIGGER. The trunk started to crack. Green ooze leaked out. The Tree Surpriser screamed a horrible wooden scream...
CRACK... CRACK... CRACK...
BOOOOOOM!!! The Belly Bonker EXPLODED out of the Tree Surpriser!!! The tree blew apart into a million pieces! Wood and green slime flew everywhere! The Belly Bonker ripped it apart from the INSIDE!
The Tree Surpriser was DEAD.
The Tree Surpriser lay on the ground, split open and oozing. The Belly Bonker stood over it, belly eye glowing with victory. But then the Spider Scope looked through its scope and saw something that made its blood run cold...
The other trees were moving.
THEY WERE ALL TREE SURPRISERS. Every single tree had glowing eyes and mouths full of teeth. HUNDREDS of them. ALL waking up. ALL hungry. The Spider Scope and Belly Bonker were completely SURROUNDED.
BOOOOM. BOOOOM. BOOOOM. The ground cracked open. Something MASSIVE crawled out. A GIANT ZOMBIE the size of a building! Rotting green flesh, glowing red eyes, bones sticking through skin. It opened its mouth and let out a roar so loud it shook every tree in the forest...
The Giant Zombie swung its enormous fists and SMASHED through the Tree Surprisers like they were toothpicks! Trees exploded! Green ooze sprayed everywhere! The Spider Scope and the Belly Bonker hid behind a rock and watched the zombie tear through the forest like a wrecking ball!
The Spider Scope, the Belly Bonker, and the Giant Zombie looked at each other. They used to be enemies. But now they had a bigger problem. The Belly Bonker's eye glowed. The Spider Scope aimed its scope. The zombie cracked its knuckles. They charged into battle TOGETHER and smashed every last Tree Surpriser into sawdust!
When it was finally over, every Tree Surpriser was gone. The forest was covered in green slime and splinters. The first light of sunrise crept through the broken trees. The Giant Zombie let out one last roar, then sank back into the ground. The Belly Bonker's eye dimmed to a soft glow. And the Spider Scope looked through its magical scope one last time... just to make sure nothing else was hiding.
The forest was quiet again. The Spider Scope crawled to the top of the tallest hill and kept watch. The Rock Person settled back into the ground, invisible as always. And somewhere deep below, the Belly Bonker and the Giant Zombie slept, waiting for the next time the forest needed them.

THE END A story by Evan
