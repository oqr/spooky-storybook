In the darkest part of the world, where the sun never shines, there is a forest that no one goes into. The trees drip with black slime. And if you listen closely... you can hear something breathing.
Deep in the shadows crawled the Spider Scope. A twisted purple spider with a magical telescope scope bolted to its head. The scope could see things hidden from every other creature, especially the Rock Person, who was invisible to everyone else.
Then the ground started to SHAKE. The Belly Bonker stepped out of the darkness. HUGE. Dark shaggy fur, claws that could tear through rock, and in its belly... a glowing green EYE that never blinked. That eye could see almost anything. But there was ONE thing it could never see: the Rock Person.
One terrible night, the Belly Bonker came crashing through the trees! Its belly eye burned bright green. It wanted to DESTROY the Spider Scope! It swung its massive claws. Trees shattered. The Spider Scope tried to run, but the Belly Bonker was too fast!
But one of the trees behind it started to move. Bark cracked. Eyes appeared. Then a mouth. Then ANOTHER mouth. Then THREE mouths, all full of teeth sharp as swords. The Tree Surpriser. But it wasn't looking at the Spider Scope. It was looking at the Belly Bonker.
The Spider Scope aimed its magical scope into the darkness and found the Rock Person hiding right next to the Belly Bonker! "HELP ME!" screamed the Spider Scope. The Rock Person could hear everything, but the Belly Bonker couldn't see it. The Rock Person was completely invisible.
SURPRISE!!! The Tree Surpriser opened ALL THREE of its horrible mouths and CHOMPED down on the Belly Bonker! The whole Belly Bonker disappeared inside the tree! Swallowed whole! The Tree Surpriser had saved the Spider Scope!

But then... the Tree Surpriser started to shake.
The Rock Person rolled right under the Belly Bonker's feet! The Belly Bonker couldn't see it coming. It TRIPPED and went flying through the air, crashing straight into the biggest tree in the forest. But that wasn't a tree... it was the Tree Surpriser. And the Tree Surpriser was VERY, VERY hungry...
Something was WRONG. Inside the Tree Surpriser, the Belly Bonker's eye glowed brighter and brighter. It wasn't dying. It was getting BIGGER. The trunk started to crack. Green ooze leaked out. The Tree Surpriser screamed a horrible wooden scream...
CRACK... CRACK... CRACK...
BOOOOOOM!!! The Belly Bonker's belly eye glowed so bright it BLASTED a hole right through the Tree Surpriser! The Belly Bonker burst out of the trunk and tumbled across the ground! Wood and green slime flew everywhere!
The Tree Surpriser was cracked open and oozing... but it was still ALIVE.
The Tree Surpriser lay on the ground, cracked and oozing green sap. But its eyes were still open. It was healing. The Belly Bonker backed away, belly eye flickering. Then the Spider Scope looked through its scope and saw something amazing...
The other trees were moving.
THEY WERE ALL TREE SURPRISERS. Every single tree had glowing eyes and mouths full of teeth. HUNDREDS of them. ALL waking up. But they weren't angry at the Spider Scope. They were protecting it. The whole forest turned to face the Belly Bonker.
BOOOOM. BOOOOM. BOOOOM. The ground cracked open. Something MASSIVE crawled out. A GIANT ZOMBIE the size of a building! Rotting green flesh, glowing red eyes, bones sticking through skin. It opened its mouth and let out a roar so loud it shook every tree in the forest...
The Tree Surprisers charged at the Giant Zombie! Branches whipped and mouths chomped! The zombie swung its fists and SMASHED through them, but more kept coming! The Spider Scope watched from behind a rock as the whole forest fought the zombie like an army of wooden soldiers!
The Spider Scope aimed its scope. The Tree Surprisers opened all their mouths. Even the Belly Bonker turned its belly eye toward the zombie. They all had a bigger problem now. The Spider Scope spotted the zombie's weak spot. The Tree Surprisers wrapped their roots around its legs. The Belly Bonker blasted it with its eye. They charged into battle TOGETHER and brought the Giant Zombie crashing down!
When it was finally over, the Giant Zombie sank back into the ground. The forest was covered in green slime and rubble. The first light of sunrise crept through the trees. The Tree Surprisers settled back into the earth, their mouths closing, their eyes dimming, looking like ordinary trees again. The Belly Bonker's eye dimmed to a soft glow. And the Spider Scope looked through its magical scope one last time... just to make sure nothing else was hiding.
The forest was quiet again. The Spider Scope crawled to the top of the tallest hill and kept watch, surrounded by the Tree Surprisers standing guard like silent wooden soldiers. The Rock Person settled back into the ground, invisible as always. And somewhere deep below, the Belly Bonker slept with one eye open, waiting for the next time the forest needed it.

THE END A story by Evan
THE NEXT DAY... The sun went down and the forest got dark again. The Slime Blob, the Terradaxoar, and Giant Legs woke up. They were STARVING. And they wanted to eat some Tree Surprisers.
There were TOO MANY Tree Surprisers! Everywhere they looked, trees with glowing eyes and mouths full of teeth. The Slime Blob only managed to eat ONE. But then they spotted one Tree Surpriser off by itself. It wasn't ready to battle. It was just standing there, sleepy...
The Slime Blob, the Terradaxoar, and Giant Legs all JUMPED on the sleepy Tree Surpriser! It didn't even have time to open its mouths! They tore it apart and ate every last branch! But there were still MORE trees to eat. So they kept going. And going. And GOING.
They ate EVERY. SINGLE. TREE SURPRISER. Their bellies were so full they could barely move. The Slime Blob was round as a basketball. Giant Legs could barely stand. The Terradaxoar couldn't even fly. And then...
THEY EXPLODED!!! Slime and wood and legs and eyeballs went EVERYWHERE.
BOOOOM. The ground cracked open and the Belly Bonker crawled out! Its belly eye glowed bright green. It looked around at the mess. Bits of Slime Blob, Terradaxoar pieces, and Giant Legs parts were scattered everywhere.

Then a GIANT ZOMBIE stomped over and ate what was left of all three of them! Swallowed the Slime Blob, the Terradaxoar, and the Giant Legs in three huge bites!
But inside the zombie's belly, something was happening. Giant Legs was changing. It used to have two legs. Now it was growing more. Three... four... FIVE LEGS!!! With five legs, Giant Legs could poop and pee out a TON of stuff at the same time. That meant it NEVER got hungry. Ever. It burst right out of the zombie!
BOOM. BOOM. BOOM. BOOM. BOOM. The ground cracked open in TEN different places. TEN MORE GIANT ZOMBIES crawled out of the earth! They stomped over to the first zombie and they all looked at each other. They were a TEAM. ELEVEN ZOMBIES.
The eleven zombies turned toward the Belly Bonker. They wanted to FEAST. But wait... the Belly Bonker's eye looked up at them. They remembered. The Belly Bonker and the zombies were good friends. They couldn't eat a friend!

So the eleven zombies needed to find something ELSE to eat.
There was still ONE more Tree Surpriser. The BIGGEST ONE in the entire forest. Taller than a skyscraper. Roots as thick as cars. And it had more mouths than any tree they'd ever seen. But eleven zombies didn't care. They CHARGED.
The eleven zombies tore into the biggest Tree Surpriser from every side! They ripped branches, chewed roots, and swallowed everything. The Belly Bonker watched from a safe distance, belly eye glowing softly. When it was over, the biggest tree in the forest was GONE.

The zombies let out eleven ROARS that shook the whole world. Then they sank back into the ground, one by one. The Belly Bonker's eye dimmed. The forest was empty and quiet.
